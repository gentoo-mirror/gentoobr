Title: LLVM and Polly moved to clang-musl overlay
Author: Leonardo Neumann <leonardo@neumann.dev.br>
Posted: 2022-03-16
Revision: 1
News-Item-Format: 2.0
Display-If-Installed: sys-devel/llvm
Display-If-Installed: sys-libs/polly

From March 16, both sys-libs/polly and sys-devel/llvm with polly useflag will
now be maintained at https://github.com/clang-musl-overlay/clang-musl-overlay
repository. Despite the clang-musl overlay purpose, both packages will continue
to support other toolchains.
